Frog Hunt! A science game where you are a predator!

Your instincts are telling you to eat frogs. However, you just moved to a new habitat where some frogs are toxic and some are not.

The frog shown below was your first prey, and it made you really sick! Remember it. You will need to determine if your future prey are toxic or not, based on that first encounter.

Click the Toxic button if you think your prey is toxic. Click the Not Toxic button if you think it is safe to eat.

You will need to decide quickly! If you take too long before deciding, your meal will jump away and escape.

While the frogs you will soon encounter are real examples of toxic vs. palatable species, the aim of this study is to determine if you can guess which ones are which, based on limited information.
